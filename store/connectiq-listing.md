## App name
faBolus

## Short summary
Remote bolus and glucose status on your wrist — the Garmin companion to the faBolus iPhone app. Experimental; not FDA-cleared.

## Description

faBolus turns your Garmin watch into a remote for the faBolus iPhone app: check your glucose, trend, and pump status, and send a confirmed bolus without reaching for your phone.

WHAT IT DOES
• See your current glucose, trend arrow, and recent history at a glance.
• Review active alerts.
• Request a bolus from your wrist, with an on-watch confirmation step.
• Cancel a running bolus or dismiss an alert.

HOW IT WORKS
The watch is a thin remote. Your iPhone running the faBolus app owns the connection to the pump and does the real work — every command sent from the watch is independently confirmed and clamped against your max-bolus limit on the phone before anything is delivered. The watch confirmation (tap 1 → 2 → 3 on touch watches, or a deliberate two-button hold on button watches) is a second safety factor, never the only one.

REQUIREMENTS
• The faBolus iPhone app, paired with your pump.
• A Bluetooth connection between the watch and the phone.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Hardware-validated on the Venu 3S. The app also builds for other touch and button watches (e.g. Forerunner 265S / 245, fenix 7) and Edge cycling computers, adapting automatically to touch vs. buttons — but those targets are not yet hardware-validated.
